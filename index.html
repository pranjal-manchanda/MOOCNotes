About Us

Taking MOOCs is a great way to learn new things! But it is easy to forget what we have learned.

Research indicates that reviewing new knowledge is essential to integrating it into our long-term memories.

But going back through all the lectures can be tedious and confusing.

MOOC Notes aims to provide a convenient interface where users can access open source notes from a variety of top MOOCS.
